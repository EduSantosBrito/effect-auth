# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good intent.
- Keep discussion focused on the project.
- Prefer clear technical arguments over personal criticism.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated off-topic or disruptive comments.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments, issues, discussions, and pull requests that violate this code. Maintainers may also block users from participating in the project.
